# New Search Rules



- Reject candidates relying mainly on receipt stability as novelty
- Run known-pattern distance before baseline screening
- Require at least two rivals before prediction freeze
- Reject validation-process claims posing as domain mechanisms
- Reject renamed or terminology-only variants of failed candidates
- Require fresh holdout path with a concrete falsifier
- Require replay that tests the claim, not only command execution
- Reject bounded-only formal claims unless the bound is the claim
- Downgrade dense counterexample partials before promotion
- Require public inspectable data or formal artifacts
- Promote at most four candidates after all gates
- Prefer domain mechanisms over scoring heuristics
