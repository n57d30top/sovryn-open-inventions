# Limitations

- Controlled stratified splits were used for cross-target comparability; official benchmark protocols were not reproduced.
- The source-hash evidence binds local CSV exports created during execution, not complete upstream dataset history.
- The schema tool is not a semantic leakage detector.
- The metric tool is not a proof of absence of leakage.
- Strong baseline scores on small or separable datasets should not be treated as a new method result.
- The Vehicle target was replaced after a real target issue; this reduces direct coverage of the preferred image-derived target list.
