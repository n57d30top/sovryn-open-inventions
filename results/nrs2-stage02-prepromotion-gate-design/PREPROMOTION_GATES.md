# Prepromotion Gates

Every candidate idea must pass or survive downgrade through these gates before promotion.

- known_pattern_distance_gate
- baseline_resistance_gate
- rival_theory_pressure_gate
- counterexample_scarcity_gate
- holdout_feasibility_gate
- replay_feasibility_gate
- external_inspectability_gate
- safety_scope_gate
- nontriviality_gate
- falsifier_accessibility_gate
