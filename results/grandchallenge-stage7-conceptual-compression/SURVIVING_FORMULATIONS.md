# Surviving Formulations

## FORM-01
Accessible Falsifier Boundary: a claim deserves strength only when a public falsifier can decide it at the claimed scope.

Variables: falsifier accessibility, control decisiveness, claim scope, execution resolution cost, replay status.

Fails when the artifact is unavailable, when no meaningful falsifier exists, or when controls are not interpretable.

## FORM-02
Decisive Falsifier and Control Boundary: claim strength is capped by the weaker of falsifier access and negative-control decisiveness.

Variables: falsifier accessibility, control decisiveness, claim scope, execution resolution cost, replay status.

Fails when the artifact is unavailable, when no meaningful falsifier exists, or when controls are not interpretable.
