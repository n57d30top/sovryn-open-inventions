# Limitations

- The result is bounded to the selected public target.
- Aggregate metrics do not guarantee scientific truth.
- Public artifacts exclude raw logs, private data, secrets, local paths, and raw source dumps.
- No breakthrough, legal novelty, patentability, or freedom-to-operate claim is made.
- The challenger did not beat the best simple temperature baseline beyond the preregistered margin. The outcome is partial and does not support a method-superiority claim.
