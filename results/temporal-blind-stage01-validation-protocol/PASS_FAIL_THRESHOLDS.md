# Pass/Fail Thresholds

Validation-ready requires all of these:

- at least 48 blind targets executed
- false true-fragility positives <= 2
- at least 3 true-fragility candidates identified
- at least 2 candidates survive deep check
- candidate precision proxy >= 0.60
- replay caveats do not invalidate surviving candidates
- counterexample/mutation pressure does not collapse the candidate class
- accuracy-like score >= 0.72
