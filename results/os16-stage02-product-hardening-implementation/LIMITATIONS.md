# Limitations

- Release-grade means bounded class capability under safe public computational and technical workloads.
- Replay coverage is sampled across package classes, not exhaustive.
- Temporal v2 is narrowed to supported evidence-panel subtypes and remains caveated.
- Formal route hardening covers rejection, counterexample, bounded-check, and refutation routing; it does not imply positive proof availability.
- Repo reproduction remains environment-sensitive and tiered rather than a blanket full-test guarantee.
- No broad acceleration, outside-use, or discovery-validation claim is made.
