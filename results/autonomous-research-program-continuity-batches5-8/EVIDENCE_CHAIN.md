# Evidence Chain

## Batch 5: Execution-first production

Batch 5 produced three deeper execution-backed public results instead of shallow backfill. It established that external repositories, datasets, and baselines can be run and reported publicly.

## Batch 6: Reproduction ladder

Batch 6 made reproduction depth explicit. It reached ladder level 8 or 9 on three targets and preserved partial and negative findings, including dataset duplicate and zero-dimension issues.

## Batch 7: Tool-building under pressure

Batch 7 built three custom instruments only because external targets required specific evidence operations:

- `schema_provenance_auditor`
- `metric_stress_validator`
- `pytest_repro_summary`

Each tool was tested and then used on a real external target.

## Batch 8: Reuse and failure benchmark

Batch 8 reused each Batch 7 tool on two new external targets. It promoted one tool, narrowed two tools, preserved two failure cases, and selected the next research direction based on those results.
