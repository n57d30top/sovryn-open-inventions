# Claim Type Taxonomy

| Claim type | Name | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| benchmark_performance_claim | Benchmark performance claim | A claim that a model performs at a stated level on a benchmark. |
| protocol_first_evaluation_claim | Protocol-first evaluation claim | A claim that source-described evaluation changes or validates a benchmark conclusion. |
| leakage_risk_claim | Leakage-risk claim | A claim that leakage-like structure is present, absent, or explains a split delta. |
| repair_success_claim | Repair-success claim | A claim that a proposed evaluation repair improves claim safety or reduces fragility. |
| repo_test_reproduction_claim | Repo/test reproduction claim | A claim that a public repo or package behavior was reproduced by runtime evidence. |
| dataset_quality_claim | Dataset quality claim | A claim about dataset schema, missingness, duplicates, target quality, or provenance. |
| time_series_anomaly_claim | Time-series anomaly claim | A claim about temporal anomaly behavior or temporal split validity. |
| cross_domain_transfer_claim | Cross-domain transfer claim | A claim that a principle or method transfers across domains. |
| conceptual_principle_claim | Conceptual principle claim | A claim about a bounded explanatory principle or concept. |
| tool_usefulness_claim | Tool usefulness claim | A claim that a tool adds value beyond simple baselines or review. |
