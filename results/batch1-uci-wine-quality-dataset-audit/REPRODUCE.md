# Reproduce

1. Download winequality-red.csv from UCI public machine-learning database.
2. Parse semicolon-delimited rows.
3. Compute row count, column count, missing cells, duplicate rows, quality distribution, and rare extreme-quality rows.
4. Compare schema-only and schema+distribution audit coverage.

Expected public outputs are README.md, SUMMARY.json, PREREGISTRATION.md, BASELINE_REPRODUCTION.md, RESULTS.md, FAILURES_AND_LOSSES.md, LIMITATIONS.md, REPRODUCE.md, and CLAIM_EVIDENCE_BINDINGS.json.
