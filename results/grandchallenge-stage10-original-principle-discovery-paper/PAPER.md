# Original Principle Discovery Grand Challenge

## Abstract
Sovryn attempted bounded original conceptual discovery by mining 100 anomalies, generating 50 principle candidates, freezing 24 non-obvious predictions, executing 18 fresh external prediction tests, designing and running six decisive experiments, and publishing a paper-style result. The surviving narrowed principle is Accessible Falsifier Boundary: a claim can be stronger when a decisive public falsifier is directly executable at the claimed scope, but falsifier access is not sufficient when negative controls or baseline gaps are weak.
