# Limitations

- Lean was not available in this environment, so Lean-route targets used fallback handling rather than fake success.
- Internal bounded finite checks are useful for finite obligations, but they do not establish unrestricted mathematical statements.
- Symbolic checks cover the registered toy statements only.
- Lemma mining remains shallow and did not close positive proof routes.
- Three checked refutations are useful negative formal evidence, not a positive discovery candidate.
- This package is useful as proof-route evidence, not as a positive formal discovery.
