# Failures And Limitations

## Failures Or Losses

The compact_geometry_vowel_score challenger lost to the simple xy2bar threshold baseline on F1, accuracy, specificity, and balanced accuracy. It is rejected rather than reported as a benchmark win.

## Limitations

- Bounded external research production run; not a claim of scientific truth.
- Public package contains aggregate metrics and curated reports, not raw logs, private data, local paths, or raw source dumps.
- Negative, partial, rejected, and tie outcomes are preserved instead of being converted into wins.
