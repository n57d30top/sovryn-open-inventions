# Evidence Ref Classification

All critical Product refs used for this public package are either public Gaia source refs or copied public-safe Product artifacts. No unresolved critical evidence refs are knowingly left in this package.

The extended validation supplement is also public-safe and included in the classification: `validate_gaia_candidate_extended.py`, `extended_validation_result.json`, and `EXTENDED_VALIDATION_TABLE.md`.

See `EVIDENCE_REF_CLASSIFICATION.json` for the machine-readable list.
