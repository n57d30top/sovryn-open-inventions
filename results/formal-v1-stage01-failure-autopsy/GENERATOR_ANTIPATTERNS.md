# Generator Antipatterns

- constant or linear rule restatement
- small-sample modular coincidence
- single-example graph invariant claim
- tautological symbolic transform
- periodic automata output without structure
- candidate without explicit falsifier
- candidate without parameter family
- candidate whose proof route is only bounded enumeration
- known recurrence with renamed variables
- holdout identical to training range
- low-complexity closed form disguised as discovery
- counterexample search deferred until after promotion
