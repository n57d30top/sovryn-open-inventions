# Limitations

- This triage only re-audits existing GBE v1 public artifacts plus bounded internal receipts; it does not execute a new external validation campaign.
- The current candidate ID has a provenance conflict between initial candidate generation, death-gate filtering, and later promotion.
- The evidence-triad idea may remain worth testing, but it cannot inherit GBE-CAND-018 without a fresh candidate card and freeze.
- Holdout and replay evidence from GBE v1 contains failures, partials, and caveats that block direct promotion.
- Mechanism pressure was plausible but underpowered and did not eliminate documentation completeness, selection artifact, or popularity baselines.
- No outside expert review was performed in this triage.
- No external-review-ready, prize-grade, breakthrough, broad intelligence, adoption, legal, medical, wet-lab, or universal truth claim is made.
