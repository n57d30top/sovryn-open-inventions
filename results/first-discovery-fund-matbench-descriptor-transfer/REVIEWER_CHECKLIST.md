# Reviewer Checklist

| Gate | Question | Evidence refs | Required outcome |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| claim_identity | Does the package preserve the exact Product candidate identity and claim without scope drift? | `FUND_CANDIDATE.json`<br>`CLAIM_EVIDENCE_BINDINGS.json#candidateId`<br>`copied-product-evidence/fund-candidate-draft.json` | Reviewer confirms the Product claim is preserved and distinguishes it from the bounded reviewer interpretation. |
| public_review_status | Is the package correctly marked as externally review-ready with major caveats, not externally validated? | `README.md`<br>`SUMMARY.json`<br>`LIMITATIONS.md` | Reviewer confirms caveat status is visible and no external validation is implied. |
| evidence_bindings | Are all Product refs either public source refs or copied into the Corpus package? | `EVIDENCE_REF_CLASSIFICATION.md`<br>`EVIDENCE_REF_CLASSIFICATION.json` | Reviewer confirms public/raw/copy/internal classification is sufficient or lists missing artifacts. |
| dataset_target | Can the reviewer identify the data source, target, measured variable, outcome, and residual? | `DATASET_AND_TARGET_TABLE.md`<br>`EFFECT_SIZE_AND_RESIDUAL_TABLE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/runtime-evidence-output-01.json` | Reviewer can state exactly what scalar result is being reviewed and what is still missing. |
| method_reproduction | Can the public inspection path be followed without private local `.sovryn` state? | `METHOD.md`<br>`REPRODUCE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/` | Reviewer records successful public artifact inspection or a blocking reproducibility gap. |
| standalone_public_reproduction | Can the reviewer run the public raw-data proxy script, and does it honestly classify exact reproduction as complete or incomplete? | `REPRODUCE_STANDALONE.md`<br>`reproduce_matbench_candidate.py`<br>`REPRODUCTION_RESULT_TABLE.md`<br>`MISSING_REPRODUCTION_INPUTS.md` | Reviewer confirms the public Matbench source loads, proxy checks run, and exact Product residual reproduction remains caveated unless missing inputs are supplied. |
| baseline_pressure | Are baseline values explicit and reviewer-recomputable in principle? | `BASELINE_RESULTS_TABLE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/runtime-evidence-output-01.json` | Reviewer accepts Product-recorded baseline pressure as inspectable, or requests raw-data recomputation. |
| rival_pressure | Are rival mechanisms explicit, and are they only scoped/weakened rather than overclaimed as eliminated? | `RIVAL_THEORY_RESULTS_TABLE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/pre-lift-claim-evidence-bindings.json` | Reviewer confirms rival caveats are accurate or identifies a stronger rival. |
| holdout_replay_pressure | Is holdout/replay support inspectable and honestly caveated? | `HOLDOUT_RESULTS_TABLE.md`<br>`REPLAY_RESULTS_TABLE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/insight-closure.json` | Reviewer distinguishes Product-bound replay/holdout from independent external reproduction. |
| counterexample_pressure | Are counterexamples and negative controls exposed without hiding the formal-object wording issue? | `COUNTEREXAMPLE_RESULTS_TABLE.md`<br>`copied-product-evidence/frozen-prediction-ledger.json`<br>`copied-product-evidence/kill-week-results.json` | Reviewer decides whether counterexample pressure is nonfatal or package-contaminating. |
| limitations_and_no_overclaim | Are limitations explicit, and does the package avoid prohibited overclaim categories? | `LIMITATIONS.md`<br>`REVIEWER_SUMMARY.md`<br>`README.md` | Reviewer confirms no Nobel, Einstein-level, breakthrough, external validation, external adoption, legal, medical, wet-lab, or unsafe claim is made. |
