# Generator Families

- graph_invariant: small graph families using degree, connectivity, radius, diameter, clique, independent set, and simple matrix invariants.
- recurrence_relation: linear, nonlinear, combinatorial, transform, and finite-difference candidates.
- symbolic_identity: polynomial, finite sum/product, bounded combinatorial, and recurrence-derived identities.
- automata_combinatorial: finite-state output sequences, word-combinatorics, grammar, and finite-state recurrence patterns.
