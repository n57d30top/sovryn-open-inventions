# External Review Scientist Paper

## Method

Sovryn implemented a receipt-backed External Review Scientist and used Field-Bound Claim Safety to mine, screen, freeze, execute, replay, calibrate, attack, and package external claim reviews.

## Results

- 500 candidates considered.
- 200 screened.
- 100 predictions frozen.
- 100 receipt-backed audits.
- 74 wrong, partial, or inconclusive predictions retained.

## Conclusion

The candidate remains promising but unproven. The strongest narrowed principle is that field specificity and actionability matter more than raw field count.
