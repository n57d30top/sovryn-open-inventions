# Domain Candidates

- Formal mathematics and bounded conjecture discovery: Formal targets have decisive counterexamples and clear proof obligations.
- Computational materials public descriptor data: Materials property residuals are high-value and public-data compatible.
- Astrophysics open catalog anomaly structure: Open sky catalogs support safe high-impact anomaly and rival-model tests.
- Climate and energy public time-indexed records: Safe public climate and energy records matter and have strong baselines.
- Scientific software reproduction fragility: Reproduction alignment is practically useful and externally inspectable.
- Temporal evaluation fragility: Temporal evaluation errors are common but previous tools remained partial.
- Safe protein structure metadata reliability: Metadata-level structure reliability is useful while avoiding laboratory-scope claims.
- Scientific dataset reliability anomalies: Dataset reliability signals can prevent false downstream claims.
- Simulation reproducibility and parameter sensitivity: Simulation parameters can reveal reproducibility boundaries.
- Benchmark methodology fragility: Benchmark artifacts are inspectable but often baseline dominated.
