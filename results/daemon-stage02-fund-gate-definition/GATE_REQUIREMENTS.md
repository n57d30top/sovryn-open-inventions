# Gate Requirements

Fund Gate requirements:

1. Candidate identity integrity.
2. High-impact safe domain.
3. Nontriviality.
4. Rival-theory pressure.
5. Baseline resistance.
6. Counterexample pressure.
7. Frozen predictions.
8. Holdout support.
9. Replay or reproduction.
10. Proof or mechanism pressure.
11. Kill Week completed with no fatal unresolved attack.
12. External review package with method, evidence bindings, reproduce instructions, limitations, and no overclaim.
