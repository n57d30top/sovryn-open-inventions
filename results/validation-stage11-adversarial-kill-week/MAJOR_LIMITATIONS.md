# Major Limitations

1. Descriptor residuals may be descriptor artifacts.
2. Receipt stability is necessary but not sufficient.
3. Package metadata checks do not replace runtime evidence.
4. Sparse public tables can make baseline residuals look stronger than they are.
5. Some replays are caveated by network and source mutability.
6. Synthetic reviewers are internal simulations only.
7. No outside-party uptake is claimed.
8. Candidate usefulness remains bounded to checked artifacts.
