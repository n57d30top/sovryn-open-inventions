- The candidate ID is collision-prone: initial card, rejected record, survivor record, and promotion record do not cleanly align.
- Execution included one wrong prediction, multiple partial outcomes, and a rival-strengthened result tied to GBE-CAND-018.
- Holdout wave reported five failures, partials, or inconclusive outcomes.
- Replay wave reported three divergence or caveat outcomes.
- Mechanism pressure remained underpowered and did not eliminate simple documentation completeness, selection artifact, or popularity baselines.
- Candidate quality, plausibility, and external review readiness scores stayed low to moderate: 57, 52, and 48.
