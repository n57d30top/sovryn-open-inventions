# Decision Preconditions

Deep validation is allowed only if all of these hold:

- One candidate ID maps to one frozen claim and one mechanism.
- No unresolved death-gate rejection exists for the promoted ID.
- Frozen predictions bind to the same candidate card used for promotion.
- Rival-theory rejection has an explicit resolution artifact.
- Holdout, replay, and mechanism caveats are low enough to justify new deep validation.

GBE-CAND-018 fails the first four preconditions in its current form.
