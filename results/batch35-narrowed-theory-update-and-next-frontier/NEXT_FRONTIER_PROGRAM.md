# Next Frontier Program

Selected: Mechanism-Guided Benchmark Repair Experiments.

Reason: The strongest next step is not more diagnosis, but testing whether protocol cards can propose safer benchmark repairs: group-aware splits, duplicate-aware filters, rare-class metric reporting, and ambiguity gates.

Rejected alternatives: Protocol Ambiguity Resolution at Scale was too documentation-heavy; Cross-Domain Transfer is premature; Repo/Test Protocol Fragility is promising but less directly connected to the frozen mechanism evidence; Time-Series Split Trials need new target scouting first.
