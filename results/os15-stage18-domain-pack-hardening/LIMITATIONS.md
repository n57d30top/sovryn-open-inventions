# Limitations

- The workload is bounded and synthetic-public; it is not a claim about all science work.
- Class-level acceleration status is limited to represented task classes and conservative baseline estimates.
- Public packages are evidence-route packages, not expert review reports.
- Temporal evaluation remains below class-level threshold.
- Package replay is sampled, not exhaustive across every package.
- No outside uptake or broad acceleration claim is made.
