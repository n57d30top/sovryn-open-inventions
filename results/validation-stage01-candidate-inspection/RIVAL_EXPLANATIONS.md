# Rival Explanations

- Data artifact: residuals reflect source formatting or curation patterns rather than candidate structure.
- Descriptor artifact: descriptor choices create the apparent residual.
- Overfitting: prior candidate selection fit small public targets too closely.
- Receipt/retrieval artifact: stable hashes may measure access stability, not scientific signal.
- Baseline insufficiency: a simpler baseline may explain most residuals.
- Random residual noise: observed residuals may be ordinary variance across heterogeneous public artifacts.
