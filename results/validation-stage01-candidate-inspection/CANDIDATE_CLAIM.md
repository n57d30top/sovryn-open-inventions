# Candidate Claim

The Receipt-Stable Descriptor Residual Candidate says that a descriptor residual is only a credible bounded discovery signal when the public-source receipt is stable under retrieval or replay, the descriptor is explicitly defined, and the residual persists against simple baselines or controls. If the source receipt changes, the descriptor is underspecified, or a trivial baseline explains the residual, the candidate must be narrowed, downgraded, or treated as not supported.
