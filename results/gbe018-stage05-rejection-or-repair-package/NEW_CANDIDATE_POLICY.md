# New Candidate Policy

Any future benchmark/protocol evidence-triad search must use a new candidate identifier. The policy is:

- Do not rename GBE-CAND-018 and continue.
- Do not treat current GBE-CAND-018 as deep-validation-ready.
- Do not use current frozen predictions as if they bind to a clean original candidate card.
- Do preserve the bounded lesson: evidence-triad completeness may be a useful seed worth a fresh test.
- Do publish the lineage repair explicitly if a future candidate rediscovers a substantially different variant.
