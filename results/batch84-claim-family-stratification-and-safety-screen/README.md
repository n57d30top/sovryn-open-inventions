# Batch 84 Claim Family Stratification and Safety Screen

Scores the 120 screened claims by feasibility, safety, execution need, replay feasibility, baseline feasibility, and usefulness. It selects 80 for prediction generation and keeps 40 reserves.

## Safety and claim boundaries
This result is public-safe, computational, and bounded. It does not claim AGI, Einstein-level intelligence, human-level science, legal validity, medical validity, external adoption, a breakthrough, or universal truth.
