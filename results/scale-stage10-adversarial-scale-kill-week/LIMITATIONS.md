# Limitations

- The run uses a bounded blind workload and does not measure open-ended real-world throughput.
- Class-level status is limited to target classes represented in this workload.
- Public packages are minimum-evidence packages, not full expert review.
- Repo, temporal, and formal classes remain below the class-level threshold in this run.
- Time-to-evidence baselines are conservative estimates and remain benchmark artifacts.
- No outside use or outside confirmation is claimed.
